Instructional Videos

  • Registration and CED 101 -- In this video, the CED Team guides you through the steps required to register for a user profile and provides an overview of the Conservation Efforts Database's features and supporting materials. Only by registering for a user profile, can you access all of the features of the CED. This video shows users where to access help documentation and how to contact the CED Team for additional support.
  • Single-Record Data Entry -- In this video, the CED Team guides users through the process to provide data into the Conservation Efforts Database. This video can help answer questions a user may have as they upload data into the CED through the single-record entry process. The CED Team takes you through a demonstration, and highlights differences a user may encounter based on the different activities and types of data collected by the CED. The CED offers an alternative collection method known as Batch Upload for those users with existing databases, but that process is covered in a separate video.
  • Batch Upload Data Entry -- In this video, the CED Team guides users through the process to populate the Batch Upload Template with information from existing databases/datasets.
  • Interactive Map and Reporting -- In this video, the CED Team guides you through the features and functions of the Conservation Efforts Database's Interactive Map as well as options for generating reports. The Interactive Map allows registered users to query and filter the data entered in the CED. In addition to user-defined queries, registered users can select various spatial layers to provide additional context to the map and area of interest. Lastly, based on the filters and resource layers selected, the CED Team guides users through the steps to generate reports available for download.
