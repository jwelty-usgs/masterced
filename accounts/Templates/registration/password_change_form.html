Password Strength

The CED requires a strong password in order to be able to enter data. There are minimal requirements for creating a password on the site. In addition, the CED requires additional security. If your password fails to meet both the site and additional CED strength requirements you will be redirected to this form and asked to strengthen your password.

Your password must:

Be at least 12 characters in length.

Contain at least one upper case letter (A)

Contain at least one lowercase letter (a)

Contain at least one number (1)

Contain at least one special character (#)
